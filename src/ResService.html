Home Repair & Renovations

Welcome to Indus View Construction! At Indus View Construction, we specialize in transforming your living spaces into functional and aesthetically pleasing environments. With years of experience in the industry, our expert team is dedicated to providing high-quality home repair and renovation services tailored to your needs. We understand that your home is your sanctuary, and we are committed to enhancing its beauty and functionality through meticulous craftsmanship and innovative design solutions.

Kitchen Renovation

Revitalize your kitchen with our comprehensive renovation services. From modern designs to functional layouts, we create spaces that inspire culinary creativity and meet the demands of today’s lifestyle.

Bathroom Remodeling

Transform your bathroom into a serene oasis. Our team handles everything from fixtures to finishes, ensuring a perfect blend of style and functionality. We offer a range of options, including luxurious bathtubs, walk-in showers, and elegant vanities, to create a space that feels like a spa retreat.

Interior Design

Our interior design services focus on creating harmonious spaces that reflect your personality and lifestyle. We work closely with you to develop a cohesive design plan that enhances your home’s beauty and functionality.

Wall and Ceiling Work

Whether it’s drywall installation, repairs, or decorative finishes, we ensure your walls and ceilings are flawless and add character to your home. Our skilled craftsmen can implement custom moldings and trims to elevate your interior aesthetics.

Marble Replacement

Upgrade your flooring and surfaces with premium marble options. Our skilled craftsmen ensure precise installation for a luxurious finish that enhances your home’s elegance. We offer a variety of marble types and styles, allowing you to choose the perfect match for your home.

Paint Work

Revive your home with our professional painting services. We offer a wide range of colors and finishes, ensuring a fresh look that lasts. Our team uses high-quality paints and techniques to achieve a smooth, durable finish that enhances your space.

Furniture Polishing

Restore the beauty of your furniture with our polishing services. We bring back the shine and luster, extending the life of your cherished pieces.

Electrical work

Our licensed electricians provide safe and efficient electrical services, from installations to repairs. Trust us to keep your home powered and secure.

Carpentry Services

From custom cabinetry to repairs, our carpentry services are designed to meet your specific needs. We craft durable and beautiful woodwork that enhances your home.
